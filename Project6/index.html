What is El Clásico?

El Clásico is more than just a soccer match—it’s a battle between two of the biggest clubs in the world, FC Barcelona and Real Madrid. Every time they face off, millions of fans tune in to witness the drama, skill, and intensity that define this legendary rivalry.

  • The term "El Clásico" refers to any match between FC Barcelona and Real Madrid in La Liga, the Spanish Cup (Copa del Rey), the Champions League, or any other competition.
  • It is one of the most-watched sporting events in the world, with over 650 million viewers per match.
  • The rivalry symbolizes regional pride, political history, and footballing dominance.
